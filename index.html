About us

CodeCafe was founded in 2023, fusing the idea of owning a cafe and enjoying programming together. It's main goal is to have a thriving community learning from each other and making programming more social and enjoyable. Along with making it easier to find jobs as a programmer and making it easier for hiring-agents to find suitable programmers.

Why?

We specifically founded CodeCafe so that people don't have to rely on funds to buy a good computer to start programming, and for people to feel inspired to start programming. It's also a way for people to find each other and learn faster.
